Lending Solutions

At Glocal BPO, we specialize in streamlining the lending process to enhance efficiency and improve customer satisfaction. We're dedicated to helping you save money and improve your lending operations. We've perfected the art of offshoring lending processes with the years of experience we carry.

Lending Process Overview

We handle every step of the lending process, from pre-qualification and loan processing to underwriting and collections, ensuring a smooth and compliant experience for borrowers and lenders alike.

  • Pre-Qualification

    Assessing applicant eligibility to streamline applications. We use a variety of methods to determine if borrowers are eligible for a loan.

  • Loan Processing

    Managing applications efficiently to maximize approval speed. Once approved, we handle the paperwork and ensure everything is processed smoothly.

  • Underwriting

    Conducting thorough risk assessments to ensure sound lending decisions. Our underwriters assess the risk and determine the terms of the loan.

  • Collection

    Implementing effective debt collection strategies to manage repayments. If a borrower doesn't pay back their loan, our team works to recover the funds within your code of conduct.

Compliance and Risk Management

We prioritize compliance with all lending regulations, including the Truth in Lending Act (TILA) and the Fair Lending Act. Our team employs robust risk management strategies to safeguard against potential compliance issues and ensure the integrity of our lending practices.

Why Choose Us?

Glocal BPO stands out as a trusted partner in lending solutions. With a proven track record of operational excellence and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we help financial institutions streamline their lending processes and enhance profitability.
